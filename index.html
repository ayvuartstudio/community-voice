Research Participation Agreement

Before you share your voice

🎓

Research purpose only

This tool is part of an academic PhD research project. Your responses will be used solely for research about place and community experience in Ōtautahi Christchurch.

🔒

Your privacy is protected

Please do not share your name, address, or personal details. Responses are stored securely and reported only as group findings — no individual will be identified.

🤖

This tool uses AI — responses may vary

The researcher does not control or predict every AI-generated response. If something feels wrong, please stop and contact the researcher directly.

📊

How your data will be used

Responses contribute to qualitative (stories, meanings) and quantitative (patterns, counts) research. Data may be published in academic work, always anonymised and in aggregate.

🚪

Participation is voluntary

You can stop at any time. If you'd like your response removed, contact the researcher and it will be deleted.
